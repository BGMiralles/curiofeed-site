curiofeed

An automated content pipeline operated by a single individual.

curiofeed selects topics from public YouTube trend data, writes and narrates short educational videos about science, history and curiosities, and uploads them to its operator's own YouTube channel, The Quiet Unknown.

Not a public service

curiofeed is not available to the public. It runs locally on its operator's machine, authenticates as that operator, and uploads only to that operator's own channel. There are no user accounts and no sign-up: the application has no users other than the person who runs it.

AI-generated content

All visuals and narration are AI-generated. Every upload is declared as synthetic media through the YouTube API, and each video is uploaded privately and reviewed by a human before any public release.

Use of the YouTube API

curiofeed uses the YouTube Data API to read public trend signals and to upload videos to its operator's own channel. It requests a single OAuth scope, youtube.upload, and does not read, modify or delete data belonging to any other account or channel. Its use of the API is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.
